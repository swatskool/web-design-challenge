Impact of Latitude on Maximum Temperature

This visualization plots the data between the Maximum Temperature of a City against the Latitude of that City. Here we observe that the temperatures increase near the equator and decrease near the poles. There is a slight difference in the trends of the two hemispheres which may be attributed to the tilt of the earth and the time of the year when the temperatures are recorded.
